Preventions Of COVID-19

SOME DO & DONT :

Do's
  • avoid contact with sick people
  • stay home if sick
  • cough or sneeze into yourelbow
  • disinfect frequently touched surfaces
  • wash your hands often
  • avoid crowds and non-essential travel
Don't
  • touch your face
  • wear a facemask unless you have symptoms
  • avoid/delay vaccination date
  • avoid use of hand sanitizer
